Destiny 2 Free Sherpa

Destiny 2 is one of my favorite video games and ever since I started playing it, it has been known for its enthusiastic community. Back in the days when I was a beginner, I met many nice players, who were always patient and willing to help out other players. As part of this awesome community, I contribute to it by doing "sherpa" activities weekly. I coach new players, help out players struggling with missions, provide guidance for those just started playing, etc.

Deman Esport Club Manager

Deman Esport Club is the biggest esport/gaming community of Chinese people here in North America. As an international student, I met many friends and had lots of fun times in this community during the early years when I got here. It definitely helped me to adapt to new environemnt, getting great help and advices, and meeting new people. I hope this community gets to help more people and maintain its original purpose, which is why I worked to become one of its managers. I host activities, deal with players conflicts, and try my best to make sure everyone gets an awesome experience here.

TAMU ENDEAVR

ENDEAVR is a nonprofit organization founded by TAMU, dedicated to futureproofing small communities, turn them into smart cities by enhancing their capacity to utilize emerging technologies, such as autonomous vehicles, ICT, IoT, and AI, to accomplish desired socio-economic, environmental, and health outcomes. I had the opportunity to contribute to ENDEAVR's research in implementing affordable, portable and less hardware dependent autonomous vehicle systems.

TAMU DEEP Program

The TAMU DEEP Program consists of graduate and undergraduate students from Physics & Astronomy and Engineering Departments, who work side-by-side with their peers and professors on research, concept, design, and fabrication of science demonstration experiments. We present these exhibits during the Festival, Shows, and other outreach program in teams of several students and faculty members.
