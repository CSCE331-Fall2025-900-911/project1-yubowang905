YOLO Object Identification (v8)

Trained a small object-detection model on a custom dataset during internship; focused on clean labels, targeted augmentation, and careful validation to reach stable performance.

Challenges

  • Handling low-light/blurred examples without overfitting.
  • Finding an augmentation mix that helped small classes.
  • Choosing checkpoints/lr for a small dataset.

Insights

  • Label consistency beats raw dataset size early on.
  • Targeted augmentations > blanket settings.
  • PR curves/confusion matrices guide real improvements.

Internal Management Tool — China Telecom

Contributed to an internal dashboard for equipment monitoring and actions. Worked across UI and data plumbing to present real-time status clearly.

Challenges

  • Presenting dense telemetry without overwhelming users.
  • Normalizing streams from multiple data sources.
  • Keeping tables and charts responsive with lots of rows.

Insights

  • Progressive disclosure (collapse/expand) reduces clutter.
  • Clear units and naming build operator trust.
  • Small UX improvements (sticky headers, quick filters) matter.
